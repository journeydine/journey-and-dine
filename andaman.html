Day by Day

The Full Itinerary

Couples ✓ Friends ✓ First-timers ✓ Adventure seekers ✓
Day1
📍 Arrival in Port Blair
Port Blair
Relaxed start · Historical evening
  • Airport pickup via private AC cab — no local transport stress
  • Visit Corbyn's Cove Beach — first taste of Andaman waters
  • Evening: Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show (entry included)
  • Overnight stay in Port Blair
The Light & Sound Show is genuinely moving — don't skip it even if you're tired from the flight. It sets the tone for the whole trip.
Day2
🚢 Cruise to Havelock Island
Havelock
White sand · Turquoise water · Dreamy sunset
  • Premium cruise from Port Blair to Havelock (Swaraj Dweep)
  • Check-in at beachside resort — ask for a sea-facing room
  • Afternoon: Radhanagar Beach (Beach No. 7)
  • Sunset at one of Asia's best-rated beaches
Arrive at Radhanagar 45 mins before sunset to find a good spot. The colours here are unlike anything you've seen on a mainland beach.
Day3
🤿 Scuba + Elephant Beach
Havelock
Best day for adventure lovers
  • Morning: Boat ride to Elephant Beach (boat ride included)
  • Boat Scuba Diving — reef starts at shallow depth, perfect for beginners
  • Complimentary snorkeling included alongside the dive
  • Hotel pickup & drop all taken care of
Even first-timers can do this — the instructors are incredibly patient. You'll be seeing coral and reef fish within minutes of entering the water. Genuinely magical.
Day4
🚢 Cruise to Neil Island
Neil Island
Peaceful · Less crowded · Island vibes
  • Morning cruise from Havelock to Neil Island (Shaheed Dweep)
  • Visit the Natural Rock Formation — the iconic "Howrah Bridge" arch
  • Laxmanpur Beach at sunset — Neil's most beautiful hour
  • Overnight stay in Neil Island
Neil feels calmer and less touristy than Havelock. It's the kind of island where you actually slow down and breathe. Don't rush the evenings here.
Day5
🌊 Bharatpur Beach + Return
Port Blair
Coral viewing · Souvenirs · Journey home
  • Morning: Bharatpur Beach — shallow water ideal for coral viewing without gear
  • Glass-bottom boat rides and snorkeling available on-site
  • Afternoon: Cruise back to Port Blair
  • Evening: Aberdeen Bazaar for shells, spices & souvenirs
Aberdeen Bazaar has the best prices for Andamanese keepsakes. Skip the overpriced hotel gift shops entirely.
Day6
✈ Departure Day
Fly Home
Return with island memories (and a sunburn)
  • Relaxed morning checkout from hotel
  • Private AC cab airport drop included in package
  • Fly home — full heart, camera roll bursting
